'Alaskar' Software

The ‘Alaskar’ software is a business solution, created for optimization of a technical maintenance process and continuing airworthiness management of aircraft used for passenger transportation, business aviation and cargo aviation. In addition to the desk-top version of the software, installed on users’ computers, the most used functions, created to monitor the technical tasks, are also provided in the web and mobile applications of the software.

Why 'ALASKAR' software?

As the main goal of the company is an introduction of up-to-date, high-tech IT solutions to the aviation sphere in order to increase the quality of aviation technical services, the most important criteria of products is their release in conformity with modern requirements. Flexible financial policy of our company allows you to purchase the software, considering individual privileges and mutual benefits.
