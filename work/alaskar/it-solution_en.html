IT-Solution for MRO

The MRO software is a comprehensive, specialized business solution for companies dealing with aircrafts maintenance, repair and overhaul. This solution is based on a long-term experience and generally accepted quality standards. The competitive position of MRO companies directly depends on their ability to reduce costs and cut down time of maintenance works, as well as optimizing business process. The ALASKAR software may help MRO companies to achieve all these ends by becoming an integral part of a MRO companies’ operating process.
