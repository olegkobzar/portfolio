Logistics and warehouse management

The Logistics and warehouse management module is a professional solution, allowing companies to manage warehouse in a more efficient way. Module functionality covers all key steps of the logistics processing: from ordering of material to release from warehouse. Such features as replenishment control, expiration management and interchangeability allowed monitoring inventories effectively. Powerful module tools facilitate quick and effective working process with large data amount, the system, in turn, makes available all necessary data, both financial and stock.

Main features:

  • Warehouse management
  • Purchase/ Exchange/ Repair orders management
  • Monitoring of the consumption and migration of materials in warehouses
  • Management of warehouse replenishment
  • Management of overdue materials
  • Material costs evaluation
  • Detailed reporting system
  • Etc
