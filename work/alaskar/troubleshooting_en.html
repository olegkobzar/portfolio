Troubleshooting Module

The module for troubleshooting is a convenient and understandable solution that allows you to register both sources and causes of aircraft malfunctions and failures, and actions to eliminate them.

Main features:

  • Malfunctions and failures registration
  • Record of recommendations for troubleshooting
  • Troubleshooting actions fixing
  • Analysis of aircraft malfunctions
  • Troubleshooting actions overview
  • Quick data search
  • Convenient reporting system

Advantages:

  • Systemize troubleshooting actions management
  • Allow sharing experience in troubleshooting
  • Simplify monitoring over troubleshooting
